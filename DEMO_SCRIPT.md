# ValueForge Demo Script (5 Minutes)

**[0:00 - 1:00] Introduction & The Problem**
*(Screen: Keep it on the Input Form, "Example 1" pre-filled but don't click submit yet)*
"Hi everyone. Most CPG brands fail not because their product is bad, but because they launch into a 'differentiation blind spot.' They look at a crowded market and guess what claim will work.
ValueForge solves this by replacing guesswork with our 3-Dimension Whitespace Model. We don't just look at whether a claim is popular—we score it across Market saturation, Consumer intent, and Brand credibility. Let me show you."

**[1:00 - 1:45] The Scan & The "Crash First" Philosophy**
*(Screen: Click "Run Analysis" on Example 1: MegaWhey Pro)*
"We're entering a concept for a new protein bar aimed at fitness millennials. We hit run, and the AI immediately goes to work."
*(Screen: The Failure Dashboard appears)*
"Notice what just happened. Before we offer *any* recommendations, we force the brand manager to look at historical failures. We use vector embeddings to match their concept against past flops. Our philosophy is: show where you will crash *before* showing where to go. You must acknowledge the risk to proceed."

**[1:45 - 2:30] The Whitespace Grid**
*(Screen: Click "I understand the risks", land on Whitespace Grid)*
"This is the heart of ValueForge. We've mapped out the entire category landscape. 
Horizontally: Market Coverage (is this space crowded at our price tier?). 
Vertically: Consumer Need Categories.
The color coding isn't arbitrary. Green is 'True Whitespace'—high consumer response, low market saturation, and high brand permission. Red means it's a bloodbath. You can instantly see where you should—and shouldn't—play."

**[2:30 - 4:00] The AI Synthesis (Value Proposition Cards)**
*(Screen: Navigate to the Value Proposition Cards page)*
"Now, we don't just leave you with a grid. We synthesize the top whitespace opportunities into actionable Value Propositions using Gemini 2.5."
*(Point to the VP Cards)*
"For each recommendation, we provide the exact LLM-generated headline and subclaims. 
But notice the 'See FOS Math' breakdown. This is not a black box. We expose exactly *why* the AI chose this: 35% Market (Tier-CDS), 40% Consumer (CRS), and 25% Brand (BPS). 
And look here—we use dynamic AI trend prediction to tell you whether this ingredient's popularity is rising or peaking, estimating your First Mover Window."

**[4:00 - 4:45] Misalignment Flags & The Deliverable**
*(Screen: Expand the "Claims to Avoid" panel, then click "Download Brand Brief")*
"If the AI detects that a claim you wanted to make is actually a poor fit for your brand's credibility, it raises a Misalignment Flag and suggests a pivot.
Finally, when you're ready, you export all of this as a printable Brand Brief PDF, ready to hand off to your product and marketing teams."

**[4:45 - 5:00] Closing**
"ValueForge doesn't just generate text—it uses a proprietary 3-axis intelligence model to guarantee your brand launches into Authentic Claim Territory. Thank you."
